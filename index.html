🔍 The Problem

Why Indian Farmers Need KisanMitra

Despite being the backbone of India's economy, farmers face critical challenges

40%

Price Loss to Middlemen

Farmers receive only 10-35% of the retail price paid by consumers. Middlemen capture the majority of value in the supply chain.

72%

Lack Price Information

The majority of small and marginal farmers have no access to real-time market prices, leading to distress sales at low prices.

86%

Small & Marginal Farmers

86% of India's farmers are small & marginal with less than 2 hectares, making them most vulnerable to market exploitation.
