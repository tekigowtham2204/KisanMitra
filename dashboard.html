KisanMitra

किसानमित्र

KisanMitra is an open-source multilingual PWA for Indian farmers with live mandi prices, MSP intelligence, weather advisories, government schemes, and direct market access.

An independent, open-source project built for Indian farmers. Not affiliated with or endorsed by any government body. Market & scheme data sourced from public APIs — eNAM, Agmarknet & data.gov.in.
